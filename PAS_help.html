Performance Appraisal Form

How to Use Performance Appraisal Form

To View the Appraisal Hand Book Click Here*

********************************* For Employees *****************************

1. Open PCC in your browser.

2. Click on Facilities.

3. Click on IMSS Human Resources.

4. Log in using your login id and password.

5. Click on Appraisals.

6. If you have already submitted the forms, you will get a screen, with 'View Appraisal' button. If not, you will get a screen with two buttons, namely, 'Project Assessment' and 'Performance Assessment'.

7.To fill Project Assessment Form:

  1. Click on Project Assessment.
  2. Fill all the information.
  3. Click on the Submit button.

Note: In Project Assessment form you can add more than one project by clicking on the Next Project button. Once you submit the form, you cannot edit the form again.

8.To fill Performance Assessment Form

  1. Click on Performance Assessment.
    2. (This form is available only after submitting the Project Assessment form)
  2. Select Employee's rating from the drop down menu. (Employee's weighted rating and Total Score are displayed automatically)
  3. Click on Submit.

Note: Once you submit the form, you cannot edit it again.

9.You can view both the forms, by clicking on View Appraisal Form.

10. After submitting Project Assessment form and Performance Assessment form, these forms are available to the Reporting Head of the corresponding Employee.

Note: After submitting both the forms, send a mail to the Reporting Head stating the submission of the forms.

********************************* For Reporting Heads **************************

1. Open PCC in your browser.

2. Click on Facilities.

3. Click on IMSS Human Resources.

4. Log in using your login id and password.

5. Click on Appraisals.

6. Now you have three options:

  • You can fill your own Appraisal Forms by clicking on Self Appraisal button. (For that, refer the steps given above for Employees)
  • You can fill the Appraisal form of group.
  • You can fill the Appraisal Meeting form of group.

7. If you wants to fill the Appraisal Form of group:

  1. Choose the name of the employee from the dropdown list, and click on Select.

    2. Note:The employee names with asterisk sign before them means, they have not yet filled their Appraisal Forms. Only select those names, which don`t have a asterisk sign.

    Note:The employee names with [R] sign after them means, they have Regular Appraisal system.

    Note:The employee names with [S] sign after them means, they have Special Appraisal system.

  2. Click on Project Assessment button. Add Manager's Comments. Click on Submit.
  3. You will get an option to fill Next Quarter Plan. Click Yes or No.
    • If you click Yes:

      • You can enter Manager's Comments and Submit. (Employee's Comments can be entered during Appraisal meeting)

    • If you click No:

      • You will be brought back to the employees Appraisal System page.

  4. Click on Performance Assessment button. Now you have to give ratings to the employee. Select Manager's rating from the drop down menu. Manager's weighted rating and Total Score are displayed automatically. Click on Submit.
  5. You will get a warning, "Are you sure you have entered comments on Appraisal Form of Employee". Click OK.
  6. You will get a confirmation that, 'Data updated Successfully'. Click OK.

Note:Now this particular employee name, which had completed both Project Assessment and Performance Assessment will move from 'Appraisal form of group' list to 'Appraisal Meeting form of group' list.

8.If you wants to fill the Appraisal Meeting form of group:(This will be done during Appraisal Meeting)

  1. Choose the name of the employee from the dropdown list, and click on Select.
  2. Add Manager's Comments, Employee will add Employee's Comments. Fill the respective confirmation dates and click Submit.
  3. You will get a confirmation that, 'Data updated successfully'. Click on OK.
  4. Following fields are required to get confirmation from the employee and Reporting Head. Employee's Confirmation: Manager's Confirmation: Employee's Confirmation Date: and Manager's Confirmation Date.
  5. After all the above mentioned fields are filled and submitted, that particular employee name disappears from the list.

Note: Once these four fields get filled and submitted, the employee and the Reporting Head cannot see any of the forms. If any of these four fields remains unfilled then the employee and the Reporting Head can edit any part of the form during the appraisal meeting.
